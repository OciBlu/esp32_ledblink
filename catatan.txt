open esp-idf: get_idf

Make New Project: idf.py create-project -p . <project-name>

Setup device:
- idf.py menuconfig (advanced setup)
- idf.py --list-targets (show chip series target)
- idf.py set-target (default setup by chip series)

Build: idf.py build

clearing the build directory: idf.py fullclean

List COM Port: ls /dev/tty*

Open Monitor: idf.py monitor

Flash to MCU: idf.py -p <COM3> -b <baudrate> flash monitor

idf.py -p /dev/ttyUSB0 flash monitor
